About Kitt

Kitt is a classically trained vocalist with a rare gift for crossing musical boundaries. From the dramatic intensity of operatic arias to the intimate nuance of jazz standards and the emotional directness of popular song, each performance is crafted with technical precision and artistic sensitivity.

With extensive training in classical vocal technique and years of professional experience, Kitt brings authenticity and excellence to every genre, creating meaningful connections with diverse audiences through the universal language of music.

Musical Repertoire

Opera

Dramatic arias and art songs from the classical and romantic traditions, performed with technical mastery and emotional depth.

  • Classic repertoire: Mozart, Puccini, Verdi
  • Contemporary operatic works
  • German Lieder and French mélodie

Jazz

The Great American Songbook and beyond, delivered with sophisticated phrasing, improvisation, and deep understanding of jazz tradition.

  • Classic standards and swing era favorites
  • Bebop and modern jazz interpretations
  • Vocal improvisation and scat singing

Popular

Contemporary songs and timeless favorites, from intimate ballads to powerful anthems, connecting with modern audiences through accessible artistry.

  • Contemporary pop and rock classics
  • Musical theater showstoppers
  • Crossover and world music selections
